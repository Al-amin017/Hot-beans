Junior Web Developer

Job Overview:

Hot Beans is seeking a motivated and detail-oriented Junior Web Developer to join our dynamic team. In this role, you will work closely with senior developers and designers to create and maintain high-quality web applications. This is an excellent opportunity for individuals with foundational web development skills who are eager to grow and contribute to exciting projects in a collaborative environment.

Salary:

£30,000 - £45,000 per year
